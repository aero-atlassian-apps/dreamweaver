## Acquisition Channels

| Channel | Strategy | Target |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Organic (Grandma Mode)** | Emotional viral loop | 40% of new users |
| **Content Marketing** | "Parenting + AI" blog posts | 25% |
| **App Store** | ASO, featured placements | 20% |
| **Partnerships** | Pediatricians, parenting blogs | 10% |
| **Paid** | Facebook/Instagram (parents 25-45) | 5% |

---

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Private Beta (50 families)

- Invite-only
- High-touch feedback collection
- Iterate on voice clone quality
- Validate sleep detection

### Phase 2: Public Beta (1,000 users)

- Open signups with waitlist
- Free tier emphasis
- Collect product-market fit signals
- A/B test onboarding

### Phase 3: Launch

- App Store submission
- PR push (parenting media)
- Grandma Mode viral campaign
- Premium tier activation
